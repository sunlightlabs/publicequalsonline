Local Groups

Local groups are the backbone of the Public=Online campaign. Our movement simply can't build a more open and transparent government without working together at the local level, and that's why we need to meet up.

Groups can work individually, in conjunction with other groups, with the Sunlight Foundation, with other organizations or all of the above.

Groups can accomplish the campaign's goals in a variety of ways. They can meet with local and national elected officials, hold events, organize happy hours or potlucks, and even serve as local spokespersons for our cause.

These are only examples. Your creativity is needed to experiment with new forms of organizing in order to win!

We will provide as much help as we can for your group through styleguides, toolkits and even financial resources. In many cases, we may even be able to help you put together events, provide talking points, teach you how to effectively lobby your elected officials, and help you be the catalyst to open up our government.
