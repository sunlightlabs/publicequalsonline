Community Projects

Local community projects are the backbone of the Public=Online campaign. Our movement simply can't build a more open and transparent government without working together at the local level, and that's why we need to work together.

We will provide as much help as we can for your projects through styleguides, toolkits and even financial resources. In many cases, we may even be able to help you put together events, provide talking points, teach you how to effectively lobby your elected officials, and help you be the catalyst to open up our government.
